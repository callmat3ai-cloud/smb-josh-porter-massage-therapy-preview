Modality Menu

Therapeutic Services

Swedish Massage

A classic relaxation technique utilizing long, gliding strokes, gentle kneading, and circular motions to improve blood flow, ease muscle tension, and melt stress away.

60/90 Mins $80+

Deep Tissue

Targeted, intense therapy using slow, deliberate pressure to release chronic muscle tension, knots, and adhesions in deeper muscle layers and connective tissue.

60/90 Mins $90+

Sports Massage

Tailored specifically to active lifestyles and athletes. Combines stretching and compression to prevent injury, improve flexibility, and accelerate muscle recovery.

60/90 Mins $90+

Prenatal Massage

Safe and nurturing bodywork designed specifically for expectant mothers to alleviate backaches, leg cramps, swelling, and fatigue associated with pregnancy.

60 Mins $85

Trigger Point

Relief focused on tight areas of muscle fibers that cause referred pain elsewhere. Ideal for chronic pain management, persistent headaches, and limited mobility.

60/90 Mins $90+

Hot Stone Therapy

Combines standard therapeutic strokes with smooth, water-heated basalt stones held in the therapist's hands or placed on specific parts of the body to deeply relax muscles.

75/90 Mins $105+

Myofascial Release

A specialized, hands-on technique targeting connective tissue (fascia) restrictions to relieve tension, eliminate pain, and restore healthy, pain-free range of motion.

60/90 Mins $95+

MEET YOUR THERAPIST

Josh Porter, LMT

Dedicated to helping Edgewood heal, one session at a time.

With years of hands-on experience and advanced training, my practice is built on a foundation of clinical knowledge and mindful intuition. I believe that effective bodywork is not a one-size-fits-all solution, but a collaborative journey toward better physical wellness.

Whether you are trying to overcome chronic lower back pain, recovering from an athletic training cycle, or simply looking to calm an overstressed nervous system, I work with you to understand your goals and deliver targeted results.

The Journey & Training

2012

The Spark & Discovery

After experiencing a significant sports injury and finding full recovery through focused clinical bodywork, Josh committed to transitioning his career to holistic manual medicine.

2014

Graduation & Licensing

Completed a rigorous 750-hour massage therapy program in Texas, finishing at the top of his class and securing his state license (LMT).

2017

Specialization Certifications

Obtained clinical certifications in Advanced Myofascial Release, Prenatal Comfort Protocols, and targeted Trigger Point Pain Management.

2021

Established Edgewood Office

Opened the private studio on Front St in Edgewood, TX, creating a dedicated haven for professional, medically-informed massage therapy.
